How it works

From public surface to verified record

1

Public-surface crawl

We crawl your published governance surface — trust pages, policy documents, AI disclosures, and compliance statements — using a scored, robots.txt-respecting crawler with a declared identity.

2

Evidence capture

Each page is captured as HTML, screenshot, and PDF. A SHA-256 hash is computed on capture and recorded to an append-only ledger with an independent timestamp. The hash is your proof of state at the moment of capture.

3

Source-bound Control Record

Findings are bound to their captured source evidence. The result is a Control Record you can present to a regulator, board, or counterparty — not a self-reported summary, but a documented chain of custody.
